Schotcheroo Cookies

Description

These are a fancier version of rice crispy treats, that include a chocolate layer on top,
and peanut butter and butterscotch flavos within.

Ingredients

  • 1 C corn syrup
  • 1 C sugar
  • 1 C peanut butterscotch
  • 5 C rice krispies
  • 1 C butterscotch chips
  • 1 C chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Melt the corn syrup and sugar together and then the peanut butter. Fold in the cereal.
    Flatten the cereal mix into a 13x9 pan and cool.
  2. Melt and mix chips together over double boiler then spread over cooled cereal mixture.
    Let harden then cut into bars serving sizes.
