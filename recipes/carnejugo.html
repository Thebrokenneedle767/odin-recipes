Carne en su Jugo

Description

Carne en su jugo is a tasty soup from Guadalajara, Mexico, that includes a rich beef stock, beans, and veggies.
The traditional restaurant has won the Guiness World Record for fastest service from ordering to served at the table.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bacon chopped
  • 2lbs chuck roast cut into 1/2 in pieces
  • 1 TB salt
  • 1 TB pepper
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 6 tomatillos
  • 1-2 serrano peppers
  • 1/2 bunch of cilantro
  • 6 C water
  • 2 T beef buillon
  • 2 C cooked pinto beans
  • cilantro for garnish
  • lime wedges
  • radishes

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon and then remove from pot and set aside.
  2. Season the beef with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the beef to the pot at a lower heat. Do not brown the meat, let the juices release.
  4. Add onion, garlic, tomatillos, serrano peppers, cilantra
    and 2 C of water to a blender. Blend until smooth
  5. Once the beef has released its juices, add the blended vegetables.
  6. Return half the bacon to the pot.
  7. Add 4 C of water and beef buillon. Stir
  8. Cover and cook for 20 minutes.
  9. To serve, add the stew to a bowl with a spoonful of cooked bans.
  10. Top with cilantro, green onions and reserved bacon.
  11. Optionally add radishes, avocado and lime wedges.
